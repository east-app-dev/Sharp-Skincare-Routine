FAQ

Good to know.

How does the skin scan work?
You hold the camera at eye level and slowly turn your head. Sharp detects your pose and captures several angles automatically, checking your lighting first so each weekly scan is comparable.
Is Sharp medical advice?
No. Sharp gives cosmetic recommendations to help you build a consistent routine. It is not a diagnosis and does not replace a dermatologist. If you have a strong skin reaction, stop and consult a professional.
What happens to my photos?
Your scan, plan and history can be backed up to your account so they sync across your devices. See our Privacy Policy for the full details on how data is handled.
How long until I see results?
It depends on your skin and consistency. Many routines show first effects from around week 2. Sharp plans run over 8 weeks, with a weekly re-scan to measure what really changes.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, one tap, any time, with no commitment. Subscriptions are managed through your App Store account.
